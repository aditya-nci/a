Key Insights

  • IMDB: Action/Adventure films dominate revenue, but Drama/Biography score higher ratings. Ratings cluster between 6-8, showing audience consensus.
  • Amazon Prime: Explosive growth post-2015, diverse directors, strong international presence (India, UK, Canada).
  • Netflix: Genre diversity, top actors and directors more evenly distributed, strong global reach.
  • Combined: Streaming platforms favor niche and international content, while IMDB remains traditional. Ratings and genre trends reflect platform strategies.

Data Cleaning & Preparation

  • Missing values handled by filling or dropping.
  • Standardized columns for merging.
  • Extracted primary genres, calculated metrics (average ratings, counts).

Conclusion

  • Genre popularity and ratings are platform-dependent, shaped by audience and recommendation algorithms.
  • Streaming platforms drive diversity and internationalization, while IMDB remains a traditional reference.
  • Methodological rigor in cleaning and merging data is essential for robust insights.
  • Future work: sentiment analysis, network analysis, predictive modeling of content success.
