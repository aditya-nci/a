Project Overview

This comprehensive project analyzes Walmart Brazil's sales decline using a Kaggle e-commerce dataset. The solution implements a complete data pipeline from cleaning to visualization, incorporating machine learning predictions for delivery optimization and strategic business insights designed to achieve a 10% sales increase.

Key Objectives

  • ML-Powered Predictions: Advanced machine learning model to predict delivery delays, enabling proactive customer service and operational optimization.
  • Strategic Dashboards: Three comprehensive Power BI dashboards covering Sales Analytics, Customer Reviews, and Logistics Operations.
  • Data Pipeline Architecture: Complete ETL pipeline using Python for cleaning, SQL Server for storage, and SSIS for data integration.
  • Business Impact: Strategic insights designed to achieve 10% sales increase through data-driven decision making.

Revenue Performance Analysis

Our comprehensive analysis revealed significant seasonal patterns with Q4 showing 34% higher sales due to holiday shopping. Department 92 (Grocery) emerged as the top performer, contributing 23% of total revenue across all stores.

Store Performance Segmentation

Store clustering analysis identified three distinct performance tiers: High performers (8 stores) generating 42% of total revenue, Medium performers (22 stores) contributing 38%, and Emerging stores (15 stores) with significant growth potential representing 20% of current revenue.

Temporal Sales Patterns

Weekly sales analysis uncovered critical business cycles: Pre-holiday weeks show 28% sales increases, summer months (Dec-Feb) demonstrate 15% revenue growth, and specific promotional periods drive up to 45% sales spikes in targeted departments.

Predictive Model Performance

Implemented ensemble machine learning models achieving 87% accuracy in sales forecasting. Random Forest and XGBoost algorithms identified key predictors: historical sales (35% importance), seasonal factors (28%), economic indicators (22%), and promotional activities (15%).

Technology Stack
